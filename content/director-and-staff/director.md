Director of ICRANet, co-founder and former President of ICRA, and promoter of the International Relativistic Astrophysics PhD (IRAP PhD), a common graduate school program of several universities and research institutes for the education of theoretical astrophysicists.

He has also been Professor of Theoretical Physics at the University of Rome "Sapienza" from 1978 to 2012.
